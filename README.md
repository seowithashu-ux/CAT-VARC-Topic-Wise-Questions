## Important Topics Covered in CAT VARC

When practicing CAT VARC topic wise questions, it is important to focus on the core areas that frequently appear in the exam.

### Reading Comprehension (RC)

Reading Comprehension forms the largest portion of the VARC section. Passages may come from areas such as philosophy, economics, psychology, literature, and science.

**Key skills required for RC:**

- Identifying the main idea of the passage  
- Understanding the author's perspective  
- Drawing logical conclusions  
- Eliminating misleading answer choices  

Regular RC practice improves both comprehension and reading speed.



### Para Jumbles

Para Jumbles test a candidate’s ability to arrange sentences in a logical sequence.

**Tips to solve Para Jumbles:**

- Identify the opening sentence  
- Look for connectors and transition words  
- Detect logical sentence pairs  

With regular practice, recognizing sentence flow becomes easier.



### Para Summary

Para Summary questions require selecting the most accurate summary of a paragraph.

**Strategy for solving Para Summary:**

- Focus on the central theme of the paragraph  
- Eliminate options introducing new ideas  
- Choose the option that captures the main message clearly  



### Odd Sentence Out

In this question type, one sentence does not logically fit within the paragraph.

**Approach to solve Odd Sentence Out:**

- Identify the logical flow of the paragraph  
- Detect sentences forming coherent pairs  
- Eliminate the sentence that breaks the sequence
